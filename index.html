Our Story

About LCYS Co.ltd

LCYS Authentic Skincare was founded in 2022 as an online store. In 2024, we expanded by opening our first local store in Taunggyi. By 2025, we officially registered as LCYS Co., Ltd. and began importing and retailing high-quality skincare and cosmetic products, providing customers with the best possible service.

In December 2025, we are further expanding our business with the launch of our perfume counter, where we will offer a curated selection of luxury fragrances. With this new addition, LCYS aims to deliver an even higher level of service and an enhanced shopping experience for all our customers.
